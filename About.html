Here are some tips for writing paragraphs: Topic sentence: The first sentence of a paragraph should introduce the main idea and not include details.
Supporting sentences: These sentences should support the main idea of the topic sentence.
Transition sentence: This sentence should relate the ideas in the current paragraph to the next.
Transition words like "first," "second," "next," "however," and "in addition" can be used.
Length: A paragraph should have between three and ten sentences.
Flow: Paragraphs should maintain a consistent flow.
